About Iglisi Watch

Family-owned since 2009. Expert watch repair, key cutting, and curated pre-owned watches — all at the heart of Durrës, Albania.

Over 15 Years of Craft

The owner began his career in 2002 working as a watchmaker for others, building precision skills before opening Iglisi Watch in 2009. Since then, our shop has been Durrës’s trusted destination for watch battery replacement, movement repair, precision key cutting — and now a curated selection of pre-owned watches, each serviced in our own workshop.

Our Workshop

Where Precision Meets Passion

Step into our family workshop — a space filled with the hum of machinery, the gleam of polished tools, and over twenty years of dedication. Every surface tells the story of watches brought back to life and keys crafted with care. Here, your timepiece and your security are in the hands of people who truly care.

The same hands that repair your watch are the ones that select, inspect and service every pre-owned watch we list for sale. When we say a watch has been checked, we mean it.

Meet the Family

Behind every watch we repair and every key we cut is the same pair of hands — the Iglisi family. Our founder spent years honing his craft working for established workshops before opening Iglisi Watch in 2009. That foundation of experience is what you feel in every repair.

We don’t just fix things. We take pride in our work, and we stand behind every repair we carry out. That’s why so many Durrës families have trusted us since 2009 — and why we’re still here, still learning, still improving.
