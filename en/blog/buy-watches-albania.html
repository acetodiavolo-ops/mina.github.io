Hislon - European Design, Reasonable Price

Hislon is a watch brand with European design that uses reliable Japanese quartz movements. They are ideal for everyday wear - accurate, durable, and equally at home in formal and casual settings.

Hislon - What It Offers

Movement: Japanese quartz, high accuracy · Price: from €50 to €130 · Models: classic, sport and dress versions · Guarantee: 1 year from Iglisi Watch

Hislon models are available in several variants: stainless steel case with a leather strap, metal-bracelet versions, and sporty versions with a rubber strap. Each model is checked before sale to make sure the movement runs correctly. For a first watch or a meaningful gift, Hislon is the choice we recommend most often.

Hislon straps are interchangeable - if you want to change the colour or material after buying, we do it in the shop through a simple process. Most swaps go through a special channel in the case and take seconds without special tools.

Navimarine - Marine Style, Albanian Price

Navimarine is a brand with a design inspired by maritime tradition, with clear dials and hour markers that stay visible even in low light. They are for people who want something that stands out - not flashy, but with clear character.

Navimarine - What It Offers

Movement: Japanese or licensed-Japanese quartz · Price: from €80 to €199 · Models: nautical, sport and special edition · Guarantee: 1 year from Iglisi Watch

Navimarine watches are larger in size and more prominent on the wrist than the Hislon models. The dials come in deep colours - blue, black and grey - with luminous markers that work in the dark. For people who spend time outdoors, at sea or in active pursuits, Navimarine is a natural choice.

Navimarine special editions - available in limited quantities - are a good option for gifts for important occasions. Contact us on WhatsApp to see what is available at the time of purchase.
