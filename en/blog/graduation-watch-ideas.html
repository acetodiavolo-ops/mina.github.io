Graduation Watch Ideas Under €200

A practical guide to choosing a watch for a new graduate: which brands make sense, which models suit which lifestyles, and how to order with delivery anywhere in Albania.

5 min read Iglisi Watch — Durrës May 2026

Why a Watch Makes Sense at Graduation

A graduation marks the transition from study to work. A watch bought for this occasion will accompany that person through job interviews, first days, meetings, and the years of professional life that follow. That kind of daily presence makes a watch more meaningful than most other gifts at this stage.

Graduates entering work also benefit practically from a good watch. Checking a watch is more discreet and professional than pulling out a phone. For anyone moving into client-facing roles, it also signals a level of attention to presentation.

Under €100: Reliable Everyday Pieces

For a university graduation where the budget is modest, the Navimarine catalogue covers this range well. These are genuine quartz watches, not cheap fashion pieces. The cases are stainless steel, the crystals are mineral glass, and the movements keep accurate time. For a student entering the workforce, a well-made Navimarine in this bracket will serve them through the early professional years without looking out of place.

The clean sport models work for graduates going into more hands-on fields. The slimmer Navimarine dress-adjacent models sit comfortably under a shirt cuff.

€100–200: The Right Range for Graduation

This is where most graduation watch purchases from our workshop fall, and for good reason. The full Hislon range sits in this bracket. Hislon is a Swiss brand with a straightforward design approach: conservative dials, slim profiles, and finish quality that is noticeably better than entry-level pieces. For a new graduate entering an office environment, a Hislon is an appropriate daily watch that will be worn consistently for five to ten years.

Entering a Professional Role

A slim Hislon Classic in polished steel with a white or silver dial is the right choice. It reads as professional without drawing attention. Works under a cuff. Pairs with formal and smart-casual equally well.

Going into a Technical or Outdoor Field

A Navimarine sport model with a 42–44mm case and higher water resistance is the practical choice. These are watches built for use, not just appearance. The bold dial layout and luminous hands make them easy to read in any conditions.

Continuing to Postgraduate Study

A versatile mid-size Hislon or Navimarine that works in both casual and semi-formal contexts is the right call. Something in the 38–40mm range with a clean dial is wearable in lectures, libraries, and social settings without looking out of place in any of them.

Moving Abroad

A watch is an especially good gift for someone moving abroad. It is compact, portable, and useful in any country. A well-made Hislon or Navimarine from Albania is something they will carry with them and be glad to have.

What to Look for in the Shop

When browsing the watch.al shop, filter by brand first. Hislon if the graduate is going into professional work; Navimarine if they are more active or outdoorsy. Within each brand, the product page shows case diameter, strap type, and movement. Choose a case size between 38 and 42mm for versatility. Below 36mm tends to read as women's; above 44mm is bold but not for everyone.
