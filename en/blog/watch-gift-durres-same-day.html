What Makes a Watch a Good Gift

A watch is one of the few gifts that is worn every day for years, sometimes decades. That is what makes it worth spending a little time on. A few things that matter:

Case size

A watch that is too large looks awkward on a smaller wrist. For women, 28–34mm cases are typically the right range. For men, 38–42mm suits most wrists. If you can tell us the recipient’s rough build, we can steer you toward the right size.

Lifestyle match

A watch for someone who works in an office every day should look different from a watch for someone who spends weekends outdoors. We stock models that suit both - describe the person and we will point you in the right direction.

Presentation

Every watch we sell comes in its original box. When you tell us it is a gift, we make sure everything is clean, presented properly, and ready to hand over as it is. A watch in a box looks like a considered gift - because it is.

Guarantee

Every watch we sell carries a 1-year Iglisi Watch guarantee. If anything goes wrong within the first year - a movement fault, a crown issue, anything related to the watch itself - we fix it at no charge. That is a real guarantee from people you can walk back to.

If You Are Not Sure of Their Taste

A watch is a personal item and not every recipient has the same preferences. If you are genuinely unsure what they would like, two options work well:

  • Come in with them. Frame it as an outing rather than a surprise - “I want to get you a watch, let’s go choose it together.” Many of the best watches gifted this way are chosen by the recipient on the spot.
  • Ask us for a store credit. We can arrange a credit for a specific amount that the recipient can use to choose their own model when they visit. Send us a WhatsApp and we will set it up.

After-work visits work well. We are open until 20:30, so coming in after finishing work is a realistic option - even on a weekday. Most people who need a gift the same day come in between 17:00 and 19:30 and leave with exactly what they need.
